  • Air Quality Measurement

  • Air Quality Measurement

Clarity air quality data in the cloud

Once the Node-S samples its first air quality measurements, the data are uploaded via 3G/4G LTE to the Clarifact Cloud, where everything is processed and stored. Access pollutant data via the Clarity Dashboard and Clarity OpenMap web apps, or retrieve measurements and sensor data using Clarifact’s REST API. There are no extra costs associated with the Clarifact Cloud. All cellular costs, data storage, and web app access are included in our service.
